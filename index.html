Giovanni Almeida is a graphic designer from Brazil who currently works and studies independently.

The pragmatism of his work is situated on the basis of understanding the constants and the variables of each creative context to operate within semantic spectrums of multiple dimensions.

Through a contemporary attitude towards the aesthetics defined by the Swiss style, he designs mainly type, posters and visual identities, while serving cultural and institutional clients.

Do not hesitate to get in contact for commissions and other information. Portfolio available upon request.
